Mensural notation note values in MEI (@dur) are: "longa", "brevis", "semibrevis", "minima", "semiminima", "fusa", and "semifusa". Note shape is not enough to define the duration of a note. The default value of a note can be modified by the context (i.e., the notes preceding or following). The modifications are two: imperfection and alteration. Imperfection consists on making a perfect note lose one-third of its value. On the other hand, alteration consists on making a note twice as long.
